# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** September 2, 2025  
**Last Updated:** September 2, 2025

## Introduction

The National Museum-Institute of Architecture after Alexander Tamanyan ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you visit our website.

## Information We Do NOT Collect

We want to be clear about what we do **not** collect:

- **Personal Information**: We do not collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, or any other personal information from website visitors
- **Cookies**: We do not use cookies, tracking cookies, or any similar technologies
- **Analytics Data**: We do not use Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, or any tracking services
- **User Accounts**: We do not require registration or user accounts
- **Contact Forms**: We do not collect information through contact forms (contact information is displayed for visitors to reach us directly)

## Information We May Collect

The only information we may collect is basic technical information that is automatically generated when you visit our website:

- **Server Logs**: Our web server may automatically record basic technical information such as your IP address, browser type, and pages visited. This information is used solely for website operation and security purposes.

## How We Use Information

Any technical information we may collect is used only for:

- Ensuring proper website functionality
- Maintaining website security
- Improving website performance

## Third-Party Services

Our website uses:

- **Content Management System**: We use Strapi CMS to manage and display museum content. This is an internal service and does not track visitors.

We do not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

## Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise share any information with third parties.

## Children's Privacy

Our website is designed to be educational and appropriate for all ages. We do not knowingly collect any information from children or adults.

## Your Rights

Since we do not collect personal information, there is no personal data to access, correct, or delete. If you have questions about our privacy practices, please contact us.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.

## Contact Information

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us using the contact information from the **Contact** page.

---

*This policy reflects our approach to providing a simple, transparent and privacy-respecting online experience for users of the Museum.*
